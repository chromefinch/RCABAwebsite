We’re Hiring RBTs!

Based in Richmond, Virginia - River City ABA is an in-home ABA provider looking for qualified, motivated, and enthusiastic RBTs to provide top quality services to the amazing individuals we serve!

About the Position

Under the supervision of our dedicated BCBAs, our RBTs provide direct, in-home ABA services to individuals with Autism and related disorders. This rewarding position can include working on social/communication skills, assisting in daily living routines, and supporting the individual with academic tasks; all while reducing behaviors that deter the client from contacting all their environment has to offer.

What We Offer

This position requires an intense level of attention to detail as data is collected throughout the course of session. River City ABA makes this easier for our BCBAs & RBTs through the use of electronic data collection. This way, we spend more time with our awesome clients and less time flipping through paper at the end of session! In addition to this user-friendly system, River City ABA also provides the following:

  • Competitive Hourly Pay (based on experience)
  • Paid Drive-Time
  • Paid Annual RBT Renewal
  • Paid Supervision
  • Flexible Schedule
  • Safety-Care Training
  • Career Advancement Opportunities

Requirements:

  • RBT certification
  • Bachelor’s degree or commensurate experience in the field
  • Reliable Transportation
  • CPR Certification
  • Clean background Check
