ABOUT THE POSITION

Based in Richmond, Virginia - River City ABA is an in-home ABA provider looking for qualified, motivated, and enthusiastic RBTs to provide top quality services to the amazing individuals we serve!

Under the supervision of our dedicated BCBAs, our RBTs provide direct, in-home ABA services to individuals with Autism and related disorders. This rewarding position can include working on social/communication skills, assisting in daily living routines, and supporting the individual with academic tasks; all while reducing behaviors that deter the client from contacting all their environment has to offer.
